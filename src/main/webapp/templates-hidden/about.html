© 2011 CoinMines Bitcoin Mining Pool CoinMines is a Frontend for a Bitcoin and SolidCoin mining pool. Pooled mining is the process of hashing the transactions that occur in the Bitcoin network. Successfully hashing a block of transactions results in a reward. These rewards are given out by a proportional algorythm which is split like this: (Reward * / Total shares this Round) * Your shares this Round. This includes the % Pool-Fee for ensuring continuning development and server housing.

Pooled mining is a way for many miners to work together in the process of hashing transactions, and then split the reward among the members based on their contribution. This gives users a steady payout, eliminating some (but not all) of the luck factor.
